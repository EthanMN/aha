Aha Speed Guide

Aha SPD depends on the SPD of Elation characters in the team.


General SPD breakpoints for Aha (MOC):

  • 120.0 SPD = 3 Aha Instants in 2 cycles
  • 133.4 SPD = 2 Aha Instants in 1 cycle
  • 142.9 SPD = 5 Aha Instants in 3 cycles

Do note that in-combat SPD buffs and debuffs on your characters also affect Aha SPD, so keep those in mind when calculating.
